* "xkbmap": setxkbmap de neo -option
* "xmodmap": setxkbmap lv && xmodmap neo_de.xmodmap

* special layer numbers:
** 0 --> undefined
** 7 --> layer 1 with Caps Lock
** 8 --> layer 2 with Caps Lock

* find current layer

|-----+-----+-------|
| '1' | 'q' | Layer |
|-----+-----+-------|
| 1   | x   |     1 |
| °   | X   |     2 |
| ¹   | …   |     3 |
| ª   | ⋮   |     4 |
| ₁   | ξ   |     5 |
| ¬   | Ξ   |     6 |
| 1   | X   |     7 |
| °   | x   |     8 |
|-----+-----+-------|
